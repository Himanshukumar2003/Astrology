Early Life

Born in a small village of Jind – Dandolan Kala in the year 1997, Gurudev Acharya Rakesh Sharma completed his schooling at Jairam Vidyapeeth, Kurukshetra. Here, after performing a sacred thread ceremony of Janeu (जनेऊ), Gurudev Acharya Rakesh Sharma undertook religious responsibility and started his quest towards 'Adhyatam'.

Education

Under the mentorship of Acharya Shri Rajesh Prasad Lekhwaar, he acquired his 'Deeksha' and gained complete knowledge on Vedic Pooja Path (वैदिक अनुष्ठान). Gurudev Acharya Rakesh Sharma completed Graduation in Sanskrit from Kurukshetra University and gained the title of 'Acharya' after completing Acharya Programme from Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi.

Spiritual Exploration

To comprehend the deeper meaning of life, Gurudev visited Rishikesh, Kedarnath, Kashi, Haridwar, Maa Vaishnodevi, and Varanasi. While residing here and serving the great Mahatmas – he gained precious and valuable knowledge of Chakra, Dhyaan, Adhyatam, Jyotish Hast Rekha Anusandhan, Face Reading Astrology, and Mantra Sadhana.

Current Pursuits

He established his first Astrology Site in Karnal with a motive to serve the community and propagate the true meaning of 'Sanatan Dharma'. Currently, Gurudev Acharya Rakesh Sharma is pursuing Ph.D. in Jyotish from Banaras Hindu University while continuing to guide seekers on their spiritual journey.
