A birth chart is a celestial snapshot of the sky at the moment you were born. It maps the positions of planets, stars, and cosmic bodies, revealing your unique astrological blueprint. This chart influences your personality traits, strengths, challenges, and life path through the interplay of zodiac signs, houses, and planetary aspects.

Your Sun sign represents your core identity and ego (what most people know as their zodiac sign). Your Moon sign reflects your emotional nature and inner self. Your Rising sign (Ascendant) shows how others perceive you and your outward demeanor. Together, these three signs form the foundation of your astrological profile.

Planetary retrogrades occur when a planet appears to move backward from our Earth perspective. Each retrograde affects different aspects of life: Mercury (communication), Venus (relationships), Mars (energy), Jupiter (growth), Saturn (discipline), etc. These periods invite reflection and revision rather than forward movement in the areas governed by that planet.

The 12 houses in astrology represent different areas of life: identity (1st), finances (2nd), communication (3rd), home (4th), creativity (5th), health (6th), relationships (7th), transformation (8th), philosophy (9th), career (10th), community (11th), and spirituality (12th). The planets in your chart fall into these houses, showing where their energies manifest in your life.
